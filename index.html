ALAGAPPA UNIVERSITY

Alagappa university is located at karaikudi in Tamilnadu is accessible from madurai Alagappa University wass brought into existence by a special act of the Government of Tamilnadu in may 1985.

ALAGAPPA UNIVERSITY COURSES

The Alagappa University courses are offeredd at the diploma,undergraduate and doctoral level The total number of courses offered at the University to 158.
